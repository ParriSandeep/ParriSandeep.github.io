About Me

My journey into Tech

Hi! I'm Parri Sandeep, a Full Stack Java Developer who made the leap into tech after earning my degree in Mechanical Engineering from IIITDM Kancheepuram.

my journey into tech has been anything but traditional. With a degree in Mechanical Engineering, I began my academic career rooted in engineering. However, my growing enthusiasm for technology and software development inspired a shift from mechanical systems to the world of coding and software.

My transition into the tech field began with an SAP MM internship, where I worked on the functional side of enterprise resource planning. This experience gave me firsthand exposure to how large-scale systems handle data and manage complex processes. I was intrigued by the logical structuring required to optimize and maintain these systems, sparking my interest in web development.

I recognized that the principles of organization and data management I observed could be applied to software development. This realization inspired me to delve into programming and web technologies, igniting my passion for creating scalable web applications. The experience was pivotal, prompting me to enhance my coding skills in Java, Spring Boot, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. These tools empowered me to implement structured logic and effective data handling, ensuring efficient and seamless user experiences on web platforms.
